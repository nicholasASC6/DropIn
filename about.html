About DropIn

DropIn is a company inspired from one of the biggest problems in schools that not many people speak on but is one of the hardest things to address when you are in that position. This position itself is usually referred to as a “dropout” in the modern day community. The idea for DropIn actually originated when my peers and I realized the issue did some research and found out that actually 7.5% of New York City students dropout of school. When we discovered this we decided to make a website that would help those who “Dropped-out”. The website is meant to give resources to those who did or were not able to finish High School. Through the website we provide students with connections to other companies, people, or schools that offer them different resources ranging from scholarships, job opportunities to even GED programs.
